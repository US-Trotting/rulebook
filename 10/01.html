10.01 Standards for Overnight Events.

The Race Secretary should prescribe standards to determine whether a horse is qualified to race in overnight events at a meeting. Where time standards are established at a meeting for both trotters and pacers, trotters shall be given a minimum of 2 seconds allowance in relation to pacers.
