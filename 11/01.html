Rule Book

11.01 Persons Eligible to Claim.

Any current active member of the USTA and/or other individual properly licensed to claim by the state racing authority having primary jurisdiction over the race meeting, may make a claim for any horse subject to claim at such meeting. An authorized agent may claim for a person qualified to claim in his or her own right, but only for the account of that person for whom he or she is agent.
