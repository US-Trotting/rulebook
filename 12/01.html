Rule Book

12.01 Making a Nomination.

All nominations must:

  1. Be made in writing.
  2. Give name and address of the registered owner and lessee
  3. Give name, color, sex, sire and dam of horse.
  4. Name the event or events in which the horse is to be nominated.
  5. Futurity Substitution. - In the event that a mare nominated to a futurity fails to have a live foal the nominator may substitute a foal if the conditions so provide.
