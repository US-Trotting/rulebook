Rule Book

13.02 Entries Required for Early Closing and Late Closing Events.

In early closing events or late closing events, if five or more horses are entered in to start, the race must be contested, except when declared off as provided in Rule 15. Pari-mutuel meetings may require five separate betting interests to start. Stakes and futurities must be raced if one or more horses are entered to start except when declared off as provided in Rule 15.
