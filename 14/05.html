Rule Book

14.05 Payment of Entrance / Starting Fee.

Entrance / starting fee shall be due at time of declaration and payable not later than 1 hour prior to post time of the race to be contested unless otherwise specified in the conditions for the race. The entrance / starting fee will not be refunded if the horse fails to start unless horse dies between time of declaration to start and start of race. For purposes of clarification entrance / starting fee shall be defined as the payment due with declaration to start.
