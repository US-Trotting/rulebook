Rule Book

14.08 Search for Entries by Presiding Judge Before Opening the Entry Box.

Just prior to opening of the box the presiding judge shall check with the race secretary or speed superintendent to ascertain if any declarations are in the office and not deposited in the entry box and he or she shall see that they are declared and drawn in the proper event.
