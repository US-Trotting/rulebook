Rule Book

15.06 Postponement of Races / Meeting of Less than 5 Days.

At meetings of a duration of 5 days or less:

  1. Races may be cancelled and starting fees returned unless the track member is willing to add the postponed races to the advertised program for subsequent days of the meeting.
  2. At the option of management any postponed races may be contested in single mile dashes. Where races are postponed under this rule management shall have the privilege of selecting the order in which the events will be raced in any combined program. .
  3. Where a race is postponed pursuant to any of the foregoing provisions only those horses originally declared in to the postponed event shall be eligible to race. Where a race is postponed and moved to another location horses previously declared may withdraw without penalty.
