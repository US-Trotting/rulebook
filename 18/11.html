18.11 Brutal, Excessive, Indiscriminate Use of Whip.

The brutal use of a whip or crop or excessive or indiscriminate use of the whip or crop shall be considered a violation and shall be punished by a fine and/or suspension. Effective May 1, 2009 and thereafter the mandatory penalties are:
Extended Pari-Mutuel Meetings County Fairs
Days Fine Days Fine
1st Offense 3 $1,000.00 7 $200.00
2nd Offense 7 $2,000.00 15 $500.00
3rd Offense 15 $3,000.00 30 $1,000.00
4th Offense License Revocation License Revocation

For the purpose of determining the appropriate penalty under this Rule the first offense begins on or after May 1, 2009. All tracks conducting an extended pari-mutuel meeting shall publish the rules pertaining to whipping in each day's official racing program.
