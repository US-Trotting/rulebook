Rule Book

18.21 Use of Wheel Discs and Mud Fenders.

It shall be the responsibility of the owner, trainer or driver to provide every sulky used in a race with solid one-color or colorless wheel discs on the inside and outside of the wheel. In his or her discretion the presiding judge may order the use of mud guards at extended pari-mutuel tracks.
