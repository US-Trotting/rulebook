U.S.T.A. Rule Book Sample

20.04 Betting on a Horse Other than Own

No owner, trainer or driver of a horse shall bet or cause any other person to bet on his behalf on any other horse in any race in which there shall start a horse owned, trained or driven by him or which he in anywise represents or handles or in which he has an interest. However such a person may participate in multiple pool wagering on a race in which his/her horse starts if his/her horse is included in the wager only in the first (winning) position.
