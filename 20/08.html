Rule Book

20.08 Withholding Information in an Investigation

In any case where an oath is administered by the judges, Board of Review or Officer of the USTA under the Rules or a notary public or any other person legally authorized to administer oaths, if the party knowingly swears falsely or withholds information pertinent to the investigation he or she shall be fined, suspended, or both or expelled.
