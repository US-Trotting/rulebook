USTA Rule Book

20.14 Furnish Negative Coggins Test Certificate

It shall be the responsibility of the trainer of a horse to furnish to the racing office all pertinent information regarding the Coggins Test of the horse so that it may be entered on the electronic eligibility of the horse prior to the horse's first start in the current year.
