Rule Book

21.12 Prohibition Against Tubing or Dosing a Horse

The tubing or dosing of any horse for any reason within 24 hours prior to its scheduled race is prohibited unless administered for medical emergency purposes by a licensed veterinarian in which case the horse shall be scratched. The practice of administration of any substance via a tube or dose syringe into a horse's stomach within 24 hours prior to its scheduled race is considered a violation.
