22.08 Reciprocity of Penalties

All persons and horses under suspension or expulsion by any racing authority or by a reputable trotting association of a foreign country upon notice from such authority or association to the Executive Vice-President, may be suspended or expelled by the USTA. For good cause shown, the Board of Review may upon consideration of the record of the proceedings had before such state racing authority or foreign association modify or so mold the penalty imposed to define the applicability thereof beyond the jurisdiction of the state racing authority or foreign association. Whether or not a penalty has been imposed by a state racing authority the District Board may make original inquiry and take original jurisdiction in any case as provided in Sections 2 and 15 of Article IV of the Bylaws.
