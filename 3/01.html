3.01 Violations.

Any USTA member violating any of its Rules or Regulations, shall be liable upon conviction, to a fine not exceeding Five Thousand Dollars ($5,000.00) or suspension, or both, or expulsion from the USTA. , unless otherwise limited in the rules.

The conviction of any corporate member of the USTA of a violation of any of its rules or regulations may also subject the officers of the said corporation to a penalty not exceeding that which is hereinabove is provided.
