4.121 Overnight Event / Race

A race for which declarations close not more than 3 days (omitting Sundays) or less than 1 day before such race is to be contested. In the absence of conditions or notice to the contrary, all entries in overnight events must close not later than 12:00 noon the day preceding the race.
