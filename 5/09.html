At all member tracks where pari-mutuel wagering is allowed, a photo finish, head numbers and starting gate must be used. At all extended pari-mutuel meetings the track member must provide for a back-up starting gate. as well. Whenever the Judges use a photo to determine the order of finish, it shall be posted for public inspection. Photo finish equipment shall not be acceptable unless a stationary camera, mounted above the finish wire and perpendicular to the race track, is employed along with a spinner or stationary target or acceptable software application shall be used. In addition, no camera utilizing a shutter between the film and the race track during the actual finish of a race or which has a field of view greater than 12 inches at the finish line shall be considered acceptable equipment. It shall be the duty of the presiding judge to verify that the photo finish equipment is in working order prior to each racing program.

13. Payment of Dues. - If a track member fails to pay the dues prescribed by the Bylaws of the Association within 30 days of notice of the amount due, the member together with its officers and directors may be suspended from membership in the Association.
