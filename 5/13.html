5.13 Prohibition Against Illegal Use of Drugs.

The personal use of any illegal drug, medication, and stimulant, depressant, narcotic or hypnotic is prohibited. At all member tracks any individual may be required to submit to a urine and/or blood sample or to any other recognized detection test relative to the detection of the above.
