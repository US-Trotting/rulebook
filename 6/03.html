6.03 Officials at Extended Meetings.

No person may act as a racing official at an extended pari-mutuel meeting without a License valid for extended pari-mutuel meetings.

No licensed official shall be qualified to act as such at any pari-mutuel meeting where he is the owner or otherwise interested in the ownership of any horse participating at such meeting.
