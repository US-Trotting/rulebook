6.18 Identifier/Assistant Paddock Judge.

At all extended pari-mutuel meetings the track member shall employ an identifier/assistant paddock judge licensed by the USTA, whose duty it shall be to check the identification of all horses coming into the paddock, to include the tattoo or freeze brand number, color, and any markings. Any discrepancy detected in the tattoo number, freeze band number, color, or markings of a horse shall be reported immediately to the paddock judge, who shall in turn report same forthwith to the presiding judge.

The identifier/assistant paddock judge shall be under the immediate supervision of the paddock judge and the general supervision of the presiding judge and may perform other duties as outlined under Rule 6.17 when directed.
