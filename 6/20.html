6.20 Race Secretary.

The duties of a race secretary shall include but are not necessarily limited to the following:

  • (a) To be familiar with the age, class and competitive ability of all horses racing at the track.
  • (b) To classify and re-classify horses in accordance with the rules.
  • (c) To examine all declaration blanks to verify all information set forth therein.
  • (d) To check the eligibility of all horses drawn in to race and verify the horses' eligibility with the presiding judge.
  • (e) To prescribe the standards for overnight events and to prepare and publish condition sheets or condition books in accordance with the rules.
