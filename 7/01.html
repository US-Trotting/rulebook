Rule Book

7.01 Bona Fide Owner or Lessee.

Horses not under lease must race in the name of the bona fide owner. Horses under lease must race in the name of the lessee and a copy of the lease must be recorded with the USTA. Persons violating this rule may be fined, suspended or expelled.
