Rule Book

8.04 Liability of Owners and Other Persons.

All owners and persons listed in a registered racing, farm, corporation, or stable, whether incorporated or not shall be jointly and severally liable for entry fees and penalties against the registered racing, farm, corporate or stable. In the event one of the owners or persons listed in a registered racing, farm, corporation, or stable is suspended all the horses shall be included in accordance with Rule 22.05.
