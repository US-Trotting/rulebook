9.06 Information Required on Horses That Have Raced in a Country Other than Canada

No electronic eligibility will be issued on a horse coming from a country other than Canada unless the following information, certified by the trotting association or governing body of that country from which the horse comes, is furnished:

  • (a) The number of starts during the preceding year, together with the number of firsts, seconds and thirds for each horse and the total amount of money won during this period.
  • (b) The number of races in which the horse has started during the current year, together with number of firsts, seconds and thirds for each horse and the money won during this period.
  • (c) A detailed list of the last six starts giving the date, place, racetrack condition, post position or handicap, if it was a handicap race, distance of the race, his position at the finish, the time of the race, the driver's name and the first three horses in the race.
