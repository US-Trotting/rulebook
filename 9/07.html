U.S.T.A. Rule Book Sample

9.07 Horses 15 Years of Age or Older.

No horse that is 15 years of age or older is eligible to perform in any race except matinees, county fair races, or races exclusively for amateur or club drivers as sanctioned by USTA, and no electronic eligibility shall be issued for such a horse except for a performance in such races.
