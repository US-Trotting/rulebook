Rule Book

9.08 Prohibitions.

  • (a) No electronic eligibility will be issued on any horse under 2 years of age.
  • (b) No electronic eligibility shall be issued for a horse from which a positive "Coggins Test" has been reported.
  • (c) No electronic eligibility will be issued on any horse not registered with the USTA.
