Rule Book

1.01 Qualifications of Members.

The following shall qualify for membership in this association.

  1. Track Members.
    1. Persons, firms, corporations, and agricultural societies and associations sponsoring and conducting meetings other than extended pari-mutuel harness race meetings.
    2. Persons, firms, associations, and corporations conducting extended pari-mutuel harness race meetings.
  2. Individual Members.
    1. Owners of registered Standardbred or non-Standardbred horses.
    2. Officials licensed by the association.
    3. Drivers licensed by the association.
    4. Trainers licensed by the association.
    5. Corporations, associations, individuals or stake secretaries sponsoring stakes or futurities, but not engaged in conducting race meetings.
    6. Officers, directors, managers, speed superintendents, race secretaries and executive employees of corporations or associations conducting harness race meetings.
    7. Officers, directors and stockholders of corporations owning and racing horses.
    8. Farm managers or persons designated to verify breeding records.
