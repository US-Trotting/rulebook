Rule Book

1.14 Voting Privileges of Members

  1. Track members other than pari-mutuel track members in good standing.
  2. Active members in good standing on September 30th of each year shall each have one vote at the next annual district meeting of the members.
  3. Extended pari-mutuel tracks will vote as set forth in article 4.05.
