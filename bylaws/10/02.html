Rule Book

10.02 Meetings of the Full Board

The board of directors of the association shall meet annually at a time and place to be fixed by the president unless provided by resolution at the previous annual meeting of the board.

  1. The board of directors shall meet whenever called together by the president or executive vice-president upon due notice given to each director.
  2. On the written request of any ten directors, the secretary shall call a special meeting of the board.
  3. At any meeting of the board of directors other than its regular annual meeting, only the business stated in the notice of the call of meeting shall be transacted.
  4. At all meetings of the board of directors, the chairman of the board, or in his absence, vice-chairman, or if neither be present, a chairman chosen by the directors present, shall preside.
