Rule Book

3.02 Special District Meetings

Special district meetings may be called at any time by a majority of the district's board of directors.

  1. Call of Meeting. - It shall be the duty of the president or executive vice-president of the association to call a special district meeting whenever requested in writing by a majority of the qualified members of the association entitled to vote at such district meeting.
  2. Notice of Meeting. - Written notice of such special district meetings shall be made in the same manner as provided in article 3.01(b), for the notice of the annual district meeting.
  3. Business Transacted. - At any special district meeting, only the business stated in the notice of the meeting shall be transacted.
