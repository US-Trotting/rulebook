4.01 District Membership Directors

Membership elected directors shall be elected by the membership of each district at the annual meeting held in each of the districts.

  1. In district 4, any election for the sub-district 4A membership director shall take place at the sub-district 4A meeting, any election for the sub-district 4B mem-bership director shall take place at the sub-district 4B meeting, and any election for the at-large membership director shall take place at whichever of the two meetings is held first in the year of the election.
