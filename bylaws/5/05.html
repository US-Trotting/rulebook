5.05 Vacancies - Track Directors

Any vacancy of a track director who through death, written resignation, (which resignation shall be filed with the United States Trotting Association office in Columbus, Ohio), removal or other cause shall be filled by the extended pari-mutuel track members of such district by designation or election as appropriate. Provided, however, that a pari-mutuel track that has a director serving on the board of directors who was automatically seated pursuant to article 4.05 will not be entitled to vote to fill the vacancy occurring in a different track directorship seat.

If an election is required, the chairman of the district board shall call a meeting of the track members of the district for the purpose of electing a track director for the balance of the term as provided in article 5.02.
