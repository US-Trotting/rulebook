5.05 Vacancies - Track Elected Directors

Any vacancy of a track director who through death, written resignation (which resignation shall be filed with the United States Trotting Association office in Columbus, Ohio), removal or other cause shall be filled by the extended pari-mutuel track members of such district. Any vacancy occurring prior to the expiration of the term of any present track director in any district shall be filled for a term of years not exceeding the unexpired term. The chairman of the district board shall call a meeting of the track members of the district for the purpose of electing a track director for the balance of the term as provided in article 4.05.
