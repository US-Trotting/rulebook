5.06 Effective Date

These bylaw amendments shall become effective as of May 1, 2017, with the following exceptions:

  1. New elections for all track directors from district 2 and 5 shall be conducted no later than May 1, 2017.
  2. The current terms of all track directors from district 2 and 5 shall expire when the new elections for all track directors from their districts take place.
  3. The election of new track directors for the newly authorized track director positions in district 1 and 7 shall be conducted no later than May 1, 2017.
  4. These new elections in district 1, 2, 5 and 7 shall be conducted in accordance with these bylaws as amended.
  5. The terms of the track directors elected in these new elections shall commence upon their election as track directors and run until the 2020 annual meeting of the full board of directors.
  6. These effective date provisions will be part of the bylaws until the 2020 annual meeting of the full board of directors, after which they will automatically be removed from the bylaws.
