Rule Book

8.01 Right of Appeal.

Any suspension, fine, denial of membership, denial of license, reprimand, termination of office, or other penalty ordered by the president, the executive vice president or a committee designated by the board may be appealed; provided, however, that any such disciplinary action that is taken against any person by the board as a whole may not be appealed.
