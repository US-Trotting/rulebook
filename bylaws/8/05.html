Rule Book

8.05 Appeal Decisions.

Decisions of all appeals heard by a district board shall be final unless the president or executive vice president refuses to concur, in which case such decisions shall be referred for further hearing to the executive committee. The executive committee shall have the power to vacate, modify, increase or reduce any penalty, in its discretion. Decisions of all appeals heard by the executive committee shall be final, whether as an initial appeal or as a further hearing of an appeal initially heard by a district board.
