Rule Book

9.01 Executive Committee Composition.

  1. The executive committee of the full board of directors shall consist of:
    1. The president.
    2. The chairman of the board.
    3. The vice-chairman of the board.
    4. The treasurer.
    5. One director from each of the district boards, who shall be appointed by the president at each annual meeting of the full board of directors.
      1. Four of those so appointed shall be pari-mutuel track directors.
  2. The president shall be the chairman of the executive committee and the chairman of the full board shall be the vice-chairman of the executive committee.
