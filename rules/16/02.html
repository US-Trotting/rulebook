Rule Book

16.02 Scoring of Horses/Coming to Starting Gate.

All horses must parade in order in front of the grandstand unless excused by the judges. Drivers must keep feet in stirrups or any foot supports from the formation of the post parade until after passing the grandstand. During or before the parade the drivers must be informed as to when to approach the gate. The horses shall be brought to the starting gate no nearer than one -quarter of a mile before the start as the racetrack will permit. On mile tracks, horses will be brought to the starting gate at the head of the stretch. (Effective 05/01/2019)
