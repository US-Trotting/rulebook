Rule Book

17.06 Qualifications and Categories of Trainer Licenses.

Trainer licenses shall be issued in the following categories and qualifications.

  1. "G)" General – A full trainer's license to enable the holder to train horses and be programmed as trainer at all member tracks of the USTA.
  2. "L" Limited - A license restricting the holder to train only horses owned wholly by the holder of such a license and to be programmed as trainer on those horses only, at member tracks of the USTA.
  3. All new applicants for a limited license shall be required to satisfactorily complete both the written and practical examination prior to being issued a license.
  4. "CD" Conditional – A probationary license indicating that the trainer has been guilty of rule violations.
