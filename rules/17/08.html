Rule Book

17.08 Driver, Trainer Written Examination Requirements.

Applicants other than for a matinee “M” driver's license shall submit to a written examination at a designated time and place to determine his or her qualifications to drive or train and his or her knowledge of racing and the rules. In addition any driver or trainer who presently holds a license and wishes to obtain a license in a higher category who has not already done so must satisfactorily complete a the requisite written examination before becoming eligible to obtain a license in another category.
