Rule Book

17.15 Mandatory Licensing of Riders.

No person shall ride a horse in any race on a racetrack of a track member, except for exhibition contests conducted by fairs on which no pari-mutuel wagering is conducted, and for which no USTA records are kept, without having first obtained from the USTA an active membership including a rider's license. (Effective May 1, 2015)
