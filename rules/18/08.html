Rule Book

18.08 Whip Regulations / Prohibitions.

Drivers will be allowed only black whips not to exceed four (4) feet, plus a snapper not longer than six (6) inches. The following actions shall be considered as excessive or indiscriminate use of the whip (Effective 05/01/2019):

  1. All drivers must keep a line in each hand beginning when the horse is behind the starting gate and continuing through the finish of the race.
  2. Whipping shall be restricted to elbow and wrist action only and the whipping arm shall not be raised about the driver's shoulder height.
  3. Drivers shall not move their whipping arm in an exaggerated manner and the lines shall remain reasonably taut during the race.
  4. Driver shall not use the whip below the level of the shaft, forward of the race bike's wheels.
  5. Drivers shall not place the whip between a horse's legs.
  6. Drivers shall not strike another horse or driver with the whip.
  7. Drivers shall not use the handle of the whip on a horse.
  8. Drivers are permitted to use their hand or the whip in a sliding or gliding manner above the level of the shaft.
  9. Drivers shall not use the whip on a tired horse, on a horse that is not visibly responding, or when a horse is not in contention in a race.
  10. Drivers shall not use the whip without giving a horse time to respond to a previous application of the whip.
  11. No leather or unusual materials may be used. The conventional snapper shall not be knotted and tape is only permitted on the handle of the whip. All other modifications of the whip are pro-hibited.
  12. Excessive, indiscriminate, visibly injurious or abusive use of the whip is prohibited.
