Rule Book

18.26 Riding Violations

A rider who does any of the following things shall be in violation of the riding rules:

  1. Jostle, strike or interfere with another horse or rider.
  2. Cross sharply in front of a horse or field of horses or otherwise ride in a reckless manner.
  3. Swerve in or out or pull up quickly.
  4. Crowd another horse or rider.
  5. Cause confusion or interference among trailing horses.
  6. Carry a horse out.
  7. Commit any act that shall impede another horse or rider.
  8. Change course in the stretch so as to interfere with another horse or rider.
  9. Ride in a careless or reckless manner.

Effective May 1, 2015
