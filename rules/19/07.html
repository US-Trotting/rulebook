Rule Book

19.07 Horses Properly Equipped.

Horses shall be properly equipped with the following:

  1. Bridle with bit in mouth and overcheck .
  2. Rubber racing, Ladder reins or Double "French" reins.
  3. Crupper.
  4. English, monte, or jockey saddle. Overgirth on saddle with only one billet.
  5. Breast collar or buxton.
  6. Appropriate saddle pad.
  7. Head numbers, head poles, line poles and two-ring martingales are prohibited.
  8. Securing the rider to the saddle and/or the legs or feet of the rider in the stirrups, including but not limited to tying the stirrups to the girth, banding the feet in the stirrups is prohibited.

(Effective 05/01/2020)
