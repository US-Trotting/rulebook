Rule Book

19.10 Whip/Crop Regulations and Prohibitions.

Riders shall be allowed whips or crops not exceeding 30 inches in length. Urging the horse other than in front of the saddle on the shoulder is prohibited. The reins shall be kept in each hand at all times. (Effective May 1, 2015)
