Rule Book

22.03 Effect of Minor Suspension on Future Engagements

Where the suspension is for a driving violation and does not exceed in time a period of 5 days, the driver may complete the engagement of all horses programmed to race in before the penalty becomes effective. Such driver may drive in stake, futurity, early closing and feature races, during a suspension of 5 days or less but the suspension will be extended 1 day for each date he drives in such a race.
