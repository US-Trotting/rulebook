Rule Book

24.01 Timing of Races

In every race, the time of each heat or race, shall be accurately taken by three timers or an approved electric timing device, in which case there shall be one timer and placed in the record in minutes, seconds and fifths of seconds and upon the decision of each heat the time thereof shall be publicly announced or posted. Effective January 1, 2016 and thereafter, all times shall be announced and recorded in 100ths of seconds. No unofficial timing shall be announced or admitted to the record and when the timers fail to act no time shall be announced or recorded for that heat. Effective January 1, 2016
