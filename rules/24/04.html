Rule Book

24.04 Error in Reported Time.

In any case of alleged error in the recording, announcement, or publication of the time made by a horse, the time so questioned shall not be changed to favor said horse or owner, except upon the sworn statement of the judges and timers who officiated in the race, and then only by order of the District Board of Review, or the Executive Vice-President.
