Rule Book

8.01 Registration of Stallion Syndicates and Racing, Farm, Corporate or Stable Names.

Stallion syndicates and racing, farm, corporate or stable names, hereby referred to as a registered stable, may be used by owners or lessees if registered with the USTA giving the names of all persons who are involved in the stable or will use the name.
