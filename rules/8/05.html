Rule Book

8.05 Inactivity of Registered Stable.

A registered stable must have its membership renewed each year. If the stable is not renewed for a period of 15 years, it will be presumed abandoned and may be reissued to another party upon proper application. Stallion syndicate names may never be reissued.

(Effective 05/01/2018)
